Intro

I am a Machine Learning Engineer with 3 years of experience working with various modalities. Majority of my background is in Computer Vision and its applications. I have worked across the entire Machine Learning project pipeline, from doing pure research on Convex Optimization and GANs through my role at UBC, applied research from my time at Vector AI Institute on Reinforcement Learning and Synthetic Data Generation, and MLOps and CI/CD of ML models during my time at Amazon.

From my undergraduate degree, I have a solid background in Multivariate Calculus, Applied Linear Algebra, Applied Statistics, Deep Learning and Machine Learning, and Reinforcement Learning. I have extensive knowledge of the theory behind Machine Learning algorithms and professional experience and knowledge of modern Deep Learning techniques. I truly believe my background and passion has prepared me well for a field as fluid as Machine Learning and AI where one needs to be able to adapt and learn about new architectures and approaches quickly.
